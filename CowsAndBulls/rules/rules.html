    Cowbunga!!

    Rules

    1. The secret word is the word that you try to guess .
    2. You get 10 chances to guess the word .
    3. You must enter 4 letter words only.
    4. For each guess that you make a bulls and cows score will be given .
    5. If the alphabets in your guess are in the correct position as that in the secret word,your bulls score will be given depending on the number of correctly placed alphabets .
    6. It the alphabets in your guess are present in the secret word but not placed corectly ,cows score will be given accordingly .

    For instance:

    Secret word : cows
    Guessed word : crow
    bulls : 1 ( since only c is in the correct place in both the guessed and secret word )
    cows : 2 (since o and w are present in the secret word but not at the correct position )
